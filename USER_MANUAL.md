## Overview

The **StoreHub Merchant Onboarding Portal** is your one-stop platform for managing your onboarding journey from account creation to going live. This portal allows you to:

- Track your onboarding progress in real-time
- Schedule training sessions with qualified trainers
- Book installation appointments
- Upload required documents and menus
- View important dates and milestones

**Key Benefits:**
- Self-service scheduling (24/7 access)
- Real-time availability viewing
- Automatic calendar updates
- Progress tracking dashboard

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Portal

1. **Receive Your Portal Link**
   - Your Customer Success Manager will send you a unique portal link
   - Format: `https://onboarding-portal.storehub.com/merchant/[your-merchant-name]`

2. **Login Process**
   - Navigate to your unique portal URL
   - Click "Login" or "Access Portal"
   - Enter the last 4 digits of your registered phone number as PIN
   - Click "Submit"

**Accepted Phone Numbers:**
- Business Owner contact number
- Merchant Point of Contact (PIC) number
- Operations Manager contact number

**Security Tips:**
- Your session lasts 24 hours
- Always logout after use on shared devices
- Contact support if you've forgotten your PIN

## Understanding Your Onboarding Journey

Your onboarding consists of **8 stages**. You'll see a visual timeline showing your progress:

### Stage 1: Preparation
- **Status:** Initial account setup
- **Duration:** 1-2 days
- **What Happens:** Your account is created in our system

### Stage 2: Kick-off/Welcome Call
- **Status:** First contact with your CSM
- **Duration:** 30 minutes
- **What Happens:** Introduction call, requirements gathering

### Stage 3: Hardware Fulfillment
- **Status:** Equipment preparation
- **Duration:** 3-7 days
- **What Happens:** POS hardware is prepared and shipped
- **Note:** This must complete before Installation can be scheduled

### Stage 4: Product Setup
- **Status:** System configuration
- **Duration:** 1-3 days
- **What Happens:** Your Salesforce account and products are configured

### Stage 5: Installation
- **Status:** POS system installation
- **Duration:** 2-3 hours onsite
- **What Happens:** Hardware installed at your location
- **Your Action Required:** Schedule installation via portal

### Stage 6: Training
- **Status:** POS and back-office training
- **Duration:** 1-2 hours
- **What Happens:** Learn to use your POS system
- **Your Action Required:** Schedule training via portal

### Stage 7: Ready Go Live
- **Status:** Pre-launch checks
- **Duration:** 1-2 days
- **What Happens:** Final verification before going live

### Stage 8: Go Live
- **Status:** You're operational!
- **What Happens:** Start using your POS system with customers

**Important:** Stages must complete in order. You cannot schedule Training before Installation is complete.

## Scheduling Training

### When Can I Schedule Training?

You can schedule training when:
- ✅ Hardware Fulfillment is complete
- ✅ Installation is complete (or scheduled)
- ✅ At least 1 business day before your desired date
- ✅ Within the next 14 days
- ✅ On weekdays (Monday-Friday)

### Training Types

**Onsite Training:**
- Trainer visits your location in person
- Available for locations within coverage areas
- Trainers assigned based on your location and language preference

**Remote Training:**
- Online training via video call
- Available for all locations
- All trainers available regardless of location

### How to Schedule Training

1. **Click "Schedule Training" button** on your portal dashboard

2. **View Your Requirements**
   - Training type (Onsite or Remote)
   - Required language (English, Bahasa Malaysia, Chinese)
   - Your assigned trainer(s)

3. **Select a Date**
   - Use the calendar to browse available dates
   - Available dates are highlighted
   - Unavailable dates are grayed out

4. **Choose a Time Slot**
   - Available time slots are shown in green
   - Busy slots show "Not Available"
   - Training sessions are 1 hour long

**Available Time Slots:**
- 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
- 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM (lunch session)
- 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM
- 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM

5. **Confirm Your Booking**
   - Review date, time, and trainer details
   - Click "Confirm Booking"
   - You'll see a success message

6. **What Happens Next**
   - Your trainer receives a calendar invitation
   - Salesforce is automatically updated
   - You'll see the training date on your dashboard

### Language Support

Trainers are matched based on your language requirements:
- **English** - All trainers available
- **Bahasa Malaysia** - Most trainers available
- **Chinese** - Select trainers available

The system automatically filters trainers who speak your required language.

## Scheduling Installation

### When Can I Schedule Installation?

You can schedule installation when:
- ✅ Hardware Fulfillment is complete
- ✅ At least 1 business day before your desired date
- ✅ Within the next 14 days
- ✅ On weekdays (Monday-Friday)
- ✅ Before Training (if training already scheduled)

### Installation Types

**For Klang Valley, Penang, and Johor Bahru (Internal Installers):**
- **Booking Type:** Direct calendar booking
- **Confirmation:** Immediate
- **Installers:** StoreHub internal team
- **Scheduling:** Self-service via portal

**For Outside Klang Valley (External Vendors):**
- **Booking Type:** Request-based submission
- **Confirmation:** Vendor calls within 24 hours
- **Installers:** Partner installation vendors
- **Scheduling:** Manual coordination

### How to Schedule Installation (Internal - Klang Valley/Penang/JB)

1. **Click "Schedule Installation" button** on your portal

2. **Select a Date**
   - Use the calendar to browse available dates
   - Dates with available installers are highlighted

3. **Choose a Time Slot**
   - Available slots shown in green
   - Installation slots are 2 hours long

**Available Time Slots:**
- 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM
- 2:30 PM - 4:30 PM
- 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM

4. **Confirm Your Booking**
   - System assigns installer automatically
   - Calendar event created
   - Status: "Scheduled"

5. **Preparation for Installation Day**
   - Ensure location access is available
   - Have power outlets ready
   - Ensure internet connectivity
   - Someone must be present during installation

### How to Request Installation (External - Outside Klang Valley)

1. **Click "Request Installation" button**

2. **Fill in Details**
   - Preferred date (2-3 options)
   - Best contact number
   - Location address
   - Special instructions

3. **Submit Request**
   - Status: "Pending Vendor Confirmation"
   - Vendor will call you within 24 hours

4. **Vendor Confirmation**
   - Vendor discusses date and logistics
   - Confirms final installation date
   - Status updated to "Scheduled"

## Uploading Documents

### Required Documents

During your onboarding, you may need to upload:

1. **Menu Items** (Required for F&B merchants)
   - Accepted formats: PDF, Excel, Word, CSV
   - Maximum size: 10 MB
   - Include: Item names, prices, categories

2. **Training Videos** (Optional)
   - Accepted formats: MP4, MOV, AVI
   - Maximum size: 100 MB
   - Purpose: Custom training materials

3. **Business Documents** (As requested by CSM)
   - Licenses, permits, certifications
   - Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, PNG
   - Maximum size: 10 MB each

### How to Upload Documents

1. **Navigate to "Documents" section** on your portal

2. **Click "Upload Document" or "Upload Menu"**

3. **Select File Type**
   - Choose document category
   - Menu items, videos, or other documents

4. **Choose File from Your Device**
   - Click "Browse" or "Choose File"
   - Select file from your computer

5. **Add Description (Optional)**
   - Brief description of the document
   - Helps CSM understand context

6. **Click "Upload"**
   - Progress bar shows upload status
   - Success message confirms upload

7. **View Uploaded Files**
   - See list of all uploaded documents
   - Download or delete as needed

## Rescheduling Appointments

### When Can I Reschedule?

**Important Rescheduling Rules:**

**Training Rescheduling:**
- ✅ Can reschedule anytime before the scheduled date
- ✅ Must give at least 1 business day notice
- ✅ New date must be within 14 days
- ✅ New date must be after Installation
- ✅ Weekdays only

**Installation Rescheduling:**
- ✅ Can reschedule anytime before the scheduled date
- ✅ Must give at least 1 business day notice
- ✅ New date must be within 14 days
- ✅ New date must be before Training (if already scheduled)
- ✅ Weekdays only

### How to Reschedule Training

1. **Go to Your Portal Dashboard**

2. **Find Your Training Booking**
   - Shows current date and trainer

3. **Click "Reschedule Training" button**

4. **Select New Date and Time**
   - Calendar shows available slots
   - May be assigned different trainer based on availability

5. **Confirm New Booking**
   - Old event is automatically canceled
   - New event created in trainer's calendar
   - Both trainers (old and new) are notified

**What Happens Behind the Scenes:**
- Old calendar event is deleted from previous trainer
- New event created with new trainer
- Salesforce updated with new trainer assignment
- New event ID stored for future rescheduling

### How to Reschedule Installation

1. **Go to Your Portal Dashboard**

2. **Find Your Installation Booking**
   - Shows current date and installer (if internal)

3. **Click "Reschedule Installation" button**

4. **For Internal (Klang Valley/Penang/JB):**
   - Select new date from calendar
   - Choose new time slot
   - System automatically assigns installer
   - Old event canceled, new event created

5. **For External (Outside Klang Valley):**
   - Submit new request with preferred dates
   - Vendor contacts you to confirm
   - Manual rescheduling process

### Cancellation Policy

**Training:**
- Can cancel anytime before scheduled date
- No penalties for cancellations
- Must reschedule to complete onboarding

**Installation:**
- Can cancel anytime before scheduled date
- Must reschedule to complete onboarding
- External vendor cancellations require 48-hour notice

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I schedule Training before Installation?
**A:** No. Training must be scheduled after Installation is complete. The system enforces this sequence.

### Q: Can I book an appointment for today?
**A:** No. All bookings require at least 1 business day advance notice (cannot book same day).

### Q: Can I schedule on weekends?
**A:** No. Training and Installation are only available Monday-Friday.

### Q: What if no trainers are available on my preferred date?
**A:** Try these options:
1. Check different time slots on the same day
2. Try adjacent dates (day before/after)
3. Contact your CSM to discuss alternatives
4. Check if remote training has more availability

### Q: Can I request a specific trainer?
**A:** Trainers are assigned based on:
- Your location (for onsite training)
- Language requirements
- Trainer availability

If you have a strong preference, contact your CSM.

### Q: What if I miss my scheduled appointment?
**A:** Contact your CSM immediately to reschedule. No-shows may delay your go-live date.

### Q: How far in advance can I book?
**A:** You can book up to 14 days in advance from your earliest available date.

### Q: Can I change my training from Onsite to Remote (or vice versa)?
**A:** Contact your CSM. This requires updating your service package in Salesforce.

### Q: What timezone are all times displayed in?
**A:** All times are in **Malaysia Time (UTC+8, Asia/Singapore timezone)**.

### Q: Can I schedule multiple training sessions?
**A:** Standard onboarding includes one training session. Contact your CSM if you need additional training.

### Q: What if my installation takes longer than scheduled?
**A:** Installations are allocated 2-hour blocks. If more time is needed, the installer will coordinate with you.

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Cannot Login

**Symptoms:** "Invalid PIN" error

**Solutions:**
1. Verify you're using the last 4 digits of your registered phone
2. Try alternative phone numbers (Business Owner, PIC, Operations Manager)
3. Check for typos
4. After 5 failed attempts, wait 15 minutes
5. Contact support if issue persists

### Problem: No Available Dates Showing

**Symptoms:** All dates appear unavailable or grayed out

**Possible Causes:**
1. **Previous stage not complete:** Check that Hardware Fulfillment is done
2. **Outside booking window:** Can only book 1-14 days ahead
3. **All trainers/installers busy:** Try different dates
4. **Service type not configured:** Contact CSM to verify settings

**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page
- Try different dates
- Check your onboarding stage status
- Contact CSM if issue persists

### Problem: Cannot Reschedule

**Symptoms:** Reschedule button not working or disabled

**Possible Causes:**
1. **Too close to appointment:** Need minimum 1 day notice
2. **Appointment already passed:** Cannot reschedule past dates
3. **System issue:** Temporary technical problem

**Solutions:**
- Ensure appointment is at least 1 day away
- Contact CSM for urgent rescheduling
- Try again after a few minutes

### Problem: Upload Failed

**Symptoms:** "Upload Error" or file won't upload

**Possible Causes:**
1. **File too large:** Exceeds maximum size limits
2. **Unsupported format:** File type not accepted
3. **Network issue:** Connection interrupted
4. **Storage full:** (rare)

**Solutions:**
- Check file size (Menu: 10 MB max, Video: 100 MB max)
- Verify file format is supported
- Try smaller file or compress
- Check internet connection
- Try again later

### Problem: Calendar Not Loading

**Symptoms:** Blank calendar or infinite loading

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try different browser
4. Check internet connection
5. Contact support if persists
